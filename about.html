The Property

About Westend Hotel

Built in 1898, Westend Hotel is one of Matheran's most iconic heritage properties. Nestled on R.C. Church Road, next to Matheran Police Station, this hotel offers a unique blend of colonial charm and modern comfort. With a swimming pool, poolside restaurant, spa, and lush gardens, it is the perfect retreat from city life.
