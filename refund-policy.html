Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. Cancellations made 72 hours or more before the scheduled check-in time are eligible for a partial refund as per the hotel policy. Cancellations made within 72 hours of check-in are non-refundable.

2. Refund Process

Refunds, if applicable, will be processed back to the original payment method within 7-10 business days after the cancellation is confirmed. Please note that service fees or convenience fees charged by the agency are non-refundable.

3. No-Shows

Failure to arrive at the hotel on the scheduled check-in date without prior notification will be treated as a no-show, and no refund will be issued.

4. Changes to Bookings

Requests for changes to booking dates are subject to availability and may incur additional charges or price differences.
