Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Sonu ("we", "our", or "us"), an independent private travel agency, collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our website to book accommodations at Westend Hotel.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you fill out a booking enquiry form or contact us via WhatsApp. This information may include:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number).
  • Booking details (check-in/check-out dates, guest count).
  • Any other information you choose to provide (special requests).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Facilitate your booking requests and enquiries.
  • Communicate with you about your booking.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Improve our services and user experience.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with Westend Hotel management solely for the purpose of fulfilling your booking request. We may also disclose information if required by law.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information that we hold.
